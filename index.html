About me

A health practitioner that became Web Developer.

I have always been passionate for technology and logical thinking. After studying for four year and practicing for three years as a health practitioner I was looking for new challenges. Programming suggested a path to logical development with creativities, I developed an interest in javascript and played around with the program for a few months. My interest grew and I decided to embark on a course to formalise my interest and foster my passion for the symmetry and logic of programming. Hence, I enrolled in General Assemblyâ€™s Intensive Web Development Immersive course to formalise my learning.

One development I was not expecting and greatly appreciate is the enhanced interest and passion for Javascript, its libraries and frameworks, but I have also developed an interest and working knowledge of Ruby and Rails and comfortable with the back end technologies.

This course also confirmed my belief that no education is wasted. I soon appreciated the link between my previous science background and programming. I am able to bring my science studies to bear on programming, bringing clear problem identification, solving, analytical thinking and holistic understanding through the projects. Additionally, experience in working with various patients has trained me to develop my communication skills so that I can discuss matters at hand with people from all walk of life and put them at ease.
